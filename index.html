Pineapple and Ham Pizza!

This is a beautiful looking cheese pizza topped with ham and pineapples.

The hearty toppings will make your mouth water.

Ingredients

  • Premade Pizza Dough(to make it simple)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Deli Sliced Ham
  • Sliced Pineapple Chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 Degrees
  2. Layout pizza dough and cover lightly with marinara sauce
  3. Add 1 handful of Mozzarella Cheese and spread over the suace
  4. Add 1 handful of Provolone Cheese and spread over the sauce
  5. Lightly add sliced Ham and Pineapple chunks on top of cheese
  6. Now place pizza in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until preferred crispiness
  7. Once removed from oven, let sit for 5 minutes then cut and serve
