Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

This will be a delicious and tasty snack.

Please enjoy this quick and easy snack by following along with these steps.

Ingredients

  • Plain white bread
  • Any type of spreadable peanut butter
  • Any type of spreadable jelly

Steps

    Take 2 slices of any type of bread and lay them side by side
    On 1 slice of bread, spread peanut butter to desired thickness
    On 2nd slice of bread, spread jelly to desired thickness
    Finally, take both completed slices of bread and combine them together and enjoy
