Pancakes

Classic Pancakes

This recipe can make a good amount of very filling and delicious pancakes.

They will be very fluffy and can be topped with any fruit or other sweet condiment of you choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Original Bisquick baking mix
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Combine and stir all Ingredients until blended
  2. Pour slightly less than about 1/4 cupful onto hot greased griddle or pan
  3. Cook until edges are dry and then flip and do the same for the other side
  4. Top with butter, syrup or more if desired and enjoy
